## Tag descriptions
* Tag **tag** is made for an index page for a specific tag. Like **country**, **tag** will be a page describing the country tag. They are also indexed as **X_tag** to make searching easier.
* Each **tag** index page contains the description as to what is expected in the attribute table for future reference.
